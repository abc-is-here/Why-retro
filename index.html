You could jump like mario and no one would judge you!

Mario jumps with a sound 'poing' and well old people love it! But nowadays what everyone seeks is realism, everyone needs those leaves crunching footstep sounds and gun in hand.

Games like snake would send the message, 'Eat apples to grow!'

An appla a day keeps the doctor away! Snake was a game that taught us to eat healthy and grow. But now we have games that teach us to shoot and kill, games that teach us to be a bad person.

Tetris showed us how to fit into the society 'perfectly' even while being randomly cubical in shape.

Tetris was a game that taught us how to fit in, how to be perfect, how to be a part of the society. But now we have games that teach us to break the rules, games that teach us to be a rebel.

Retro games were not just games, they were a way of life, a way of thinking, a way of being. They taught us values, they taught us how to be a better person. But now we have games that teach us to be a bad person, games that teach us to be a rebel.

So let's go back to the days when life was retro!
